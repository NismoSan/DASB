## Pre-Master Quests

### 1. Tutorial
- **Level:** 1-10
- **Items Required:** None
- **Rewards:** Dirk, Boots, Shirt/Blouse, Small Emerald Ring, Small Spinel Ring, 1,000 Coins, 5,000 Experience

**Walkthrough:**
1. Receive armor and boots via popup. Press "a" to open inventory, double-click to equip.
2. Press spacebar to perform basic attacks on the little brown creatures.
3. Click on mundanes (NPCs) to speak with them. Read carefully and take note of what they say.
4. Follow the path to the tutorial exit.

### 2. Riona's Greeting
- **Level:** 1-11
- **Items Required:** None
- **Rewards:** 500 Experience

**NPC:** Riona -- Mileth Inn (Map 136), coordinates (3,3)

**Walkthrough:**
1. After completing the Tutorial, go to Mileth Inn.
2. Speak with Riona and select "Riona's Greeting."
3. Choose "Thank you for your kind words."
4. Choose "Really, I want to know."
5. Complete the dialogue.

### 3. Mundane Birth
- **Level:** 1+
- **Items Required:** None
- **Rewards:** Legend "(Town) mundane birth - (Season), Deoch, (#)"

**NPC:** Riona -- Mileth Inn (Map 136)

**Walkthrough:**
1. Speak with Riona and select "Mundane Birth."
2. Select "I have investigated my past" then "I am committed to this."
3. Choose a season for your legend mark.
4. Enter your mundane age (between 12 and 40). Example: entering 35 results in "deoch 2."
5. Select a town for your legend mark.

**CRITICAL NOTE:** This quest can only be completed correctly once. Changing selections afterward costs 100,000 Experience penalty each time.

### 4. Talk to Mileth Mundanes
- **Level:** 1+
- **Items Required:** None
- **Rewards:** 7,000 Experience

**NPCs and Locations:**
- **Torrance** -- Mileth Armor Shop (Map 133, coords 3,5) -- Topic: Arms and Equipment
- **Devlin** -- Mileth White Magic Master (Map 302, coords 5,2) -- Topics: Woods, Faerie Love, Graymere (Wizard/Priest only), Entreaties (Priest only)
- **Keallach** -- Mileth Combat Skill Master (Map 301, coords 5,5) -- Topic: Fighter Training

**Walkthrough:**
1. Visit Torrance and listen to his lecture on Arms. (1,250 XP)
2. Visit Devlin and listen to lectures on the Woods (2,000 XP) and Faerie Love (1,500 XP).
3. Visit Keallach and learn about Fighter Training. (2,250 XP)
4. (Optional) Speak with a temple attendant about the gods (registered players only).

### 23. Adoration of Danaan
- **Level:** 11+
- **Items Required:** None
- **Rewards:** "Adoration of Danaan (#)" legend, +2 all stats, +100 Health, +100 Mana

**Overview:** Takes approximately one year. Choose a deity: Mehadi, Pravat, or Astrid.

**Part 1 -- Eulogy:** Visit your deity's area. Pravat (35,15)→Pravat Cross, Mehadi Heart East (5,15)→Mehadi Rose, Astrid Center (20,15)→Astrid Octogram.

**Part 2 -- Redemption:** Upgrade items. Pravat Cross→Adranuch Cross, Mehadi Rose→Matrika Rose, Astrid Octogram→Arpina Octogram.

**Part 3 -- Adoration:** 3 players (one from each deity area) with upgraded items meet at Pravat Prole Ground (35,15). One initiates, all three receive rewards.
